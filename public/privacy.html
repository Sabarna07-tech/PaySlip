Privacy Policy

PaySlip — Mini Payroll Calculator

Data Storage

This extension stores salary, payslip, company settings, logo, and template data locally in your browser using the chrome.storage.local API. Payroll records are not uploaded to PaySlip servers. All PDF payslips, ZIP bulk exports, CSV/JSON backups, and salary register reports are generated entirely on your device and saved directly to your downloads.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect payroll data for analytics, advertising, or tracking. Specifically:

  • Payroll records, employee details, company settings, and generated payslip history stay in local browser storage.
  • If you enter a PaySlip Pro license key, that license key is sent to Lemon Squeezy over HTTPS only to validate your subscription status.
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind are used.
  • No tracking pixels, cookies, or fingerprinting techniques are employed.
  • No user accounts or sign-ins are required.

Permissions

The extension requests storage to save local payslip history, templates, company settings, and license status. It also declares access to https://api.lemonsqueezy.com/* so license keys can be validated.

Data Deletion

You can export, restore, or clear stored payslip history and templates from the extension Settings screen. Uninstalling the extension will also remove locally stored data.

Limited Use

The use of information received from Chrome APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
