Privacy Policy

PaySlip — Mini Payroll Calculator

Data Storage

This extension stores salary and payslip data locally in your browser using the chrome.storage.local API. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Data Collection

This extension collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to any server, API, or third-party service.
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind are used.
  • No tracking pixels, cookies, or fingerprinting techniques are employed.
  • No user accounts or sign-ins are required.

Permissions

The only permission requested is storage, which is used exclusively to save your recent payslip history locally so it persists between popup sessions.

Data Deletion

You can clear all stored payslip data at any time from within the extension. Uninstalling the extension will also remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
